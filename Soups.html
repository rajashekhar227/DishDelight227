Tomato Soup

A creamy and slightly tangy soup made with ripe tomatoes, spices, and a touch of cream.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cream (for garnish)

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil tomatoes until soft. Cool and blend into a puree.
  2. Heat butter, sauté onions and garlic. Add tomato puree.
  3. Simmer for 10–15 mins. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Blend again if needed. Garnish with cream before serving.

Sweet Corn Soup

A mild and comforting soup made with sweet corn kernels, veggies, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup carrots & beans (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 3 cups water or veg stock
  • Salt & pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil corn and veggies in stock till soft.
  2. Add cornflour slurry and simmer until thick.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.

Hot & Sour Soup

Spicy and tangy Indo-Chinese soup made with vegetables and bold flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage, carrot
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • Cornflour slurry

Preparation Steps

  1. Sauté vegetables on high heat for 2–3 mins.
  2. Add sauces, water, and bring to boil.
  3. Add cornflour slurry and cook till thick.
  4. Garnish with spring onion greens.

Cream of Mushroom Soup

Smooth and creamy soup with earthy mushrooms and a touch of cream – perfect comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Butter, salt & pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Sauté onions and mushrooms in butter.
  2. Add flour and mix well. Pour in milk.
  3. Simmer until creamy. Blend if desired.

Rasam

A tangy South Indian soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, and spices – great for digestion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon-sized tamarind
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp rasam powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves, coriander

Preparation Steps

  1. Extract tamarind juice and boil with tomatoes and rasam powder.
  2. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves in ghee and add to rasam.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander.
